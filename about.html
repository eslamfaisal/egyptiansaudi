About Us

Who we are

The Egyptian Saudi Financial Consultancy is the first company in Egypt supporting clients remotely to have full control over business segments such as finance, accounting and marketing in Egypt and Gulf countries on multi levels. We provide our clients smart solutions by assessing their needs. Whether it is implementation of ERP system, need of trained team to perform grunt work or highly skilled executives to guide management in making right decision during complex process of restructuring business.

We offer our clients variety of services in the area of:

Financial Consultancy and Accounting Controls

System implementation/Web Development/App Development

Business Development

Work Force Management
